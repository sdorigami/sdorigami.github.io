Rooted in a
Rich History

Our group was founded by Florence Temko, a prolific origami author, and V'Ann Cornelius, who co-curated one of the first major museum origami exhibitions in the world. The group is co-coordinated by Arnold Tubis — retired physics professor, author of nine origami books, and an internationally exhibited designer whose box design graces this very site.

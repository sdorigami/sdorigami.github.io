Meeting Information

We meet regularly at locations around San Diego. All skill levels welcome — from first-time folders to seasoned artists. No experience required, no cost to attend.

When: Update with your meeting schedule — e.g. "Second Saturday of each month, 1–4pm"
Where: Update with your location — e.g. "San Diego Public Library, Room 123, 820 E St, San Diego CA 92101"
Cost: Free to attend. Paper provided for beginners.
Email: info@sdorigami.org

New members are always welcome. Just show up — or send us a note first if you'd like to know more before coming.

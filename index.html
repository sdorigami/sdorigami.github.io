Welcome

What is the Greater San Diego Origami Group?

We are a community of paper folders based in San Diego, California — brought together by a shared love of origami in all its forms. From simple cranes to complex tessellations, from seasoned artists to first-time folders, everyone has a place here.

Rooted in a
Rich History

Our group was founded by Florence Temko, a prolific origami author, and V'Ann Cornelius, who co-curated one of the first major museum origami exhibitions in the world. Their legacy lives in every fold our members make today.
